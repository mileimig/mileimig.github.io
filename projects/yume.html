Yume Alarm

2021

:

YUME is the project of a friendly ubiquitous system for regulating and monitoring the sleep routine, made from the desire to help people regulate their sleep and facilitate the process of waking up.

The product consists of a lampshade alarm with a physical control interface, a mobile aplication for configuring and personalizing the alarm and a mat sensor for detecting heartbeat and vibrations during sleep.

Product Design, Interaction Design (UI & UX), 3D, Diagnostic Research, Benchmarking
