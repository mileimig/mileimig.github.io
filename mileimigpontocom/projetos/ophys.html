Ophys Typeface

2021

:

Ophys is a serif made up of too many ideas,

It mirrors and tries to understand traditional serif shapes, while occasionally overdoing and distorting itself in its dramatic contrast, curves and sharp ends. Ophys feels to me like waking up with a pinch. I thought about letters that could look as confident and sensitive as an orchid or a serpent, sharp as snake teeth, smooth as a flower petal.
