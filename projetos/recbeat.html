Authorial project simulating a sample of the visual identity of the 2023 Rec-Beat Festival, the 27th edition of one of the great music festivals in Brazil, which annually brings together local, national and international attractions in the carnival of the city of Recife, state of Pernambuco.

The work consists of an illustrated poster, schedule table and cards for Instagram.
