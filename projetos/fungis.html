FUNGIS Typeface

2021

:

A typeface made by combining the versatility of grotesque fonts with her curves and cut bowls in some of the letterforms, making her neutral from a distance and a bit more nuanced up close. FUNGIS is not pretty all the time, but she is friendly and outgoing, a blank canvas.

This is my second released font and she was a way for me to understand legible letterforms. She comes in 3 styles — Regular, Bold, Heavy — and has a couple glyphs missing as she’s still in development.
