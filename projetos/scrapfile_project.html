Scrapfile Project

2020

:

The Scrapfile Project was the development of a dashboard for collecting and monitoring the real time data around the volumetric measurement of metal within a scrap yard — a proof of concept for Gerdau AçoNorte in partnership with C.E.S.A.R. School.

Interaction Design (UI & UX), Prototyping, Usability Testing, UX Research, Diagnostic Research, Benchmarking
